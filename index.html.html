FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before working with MadeByLA.

How long does a typical project take? ↓
It depends on the scope. Simple tasks like image enhancement or caption writing can be done within 24 hours. Larger projects like AI persona creation or social media packages typically take 3-5 business days. I always give a clear timeline before starting.
How do we communicate during the project? ↓
We can communicate through email, LinkedIn, or whatever platform works best for you. I'm responsive and will keep you updated throughout the entire process.
Do you offer revisions? ↓
Yes — revisions are always welcome. Your satisfaction is the priority. If something doesn't feel right, I'll work with you until it does.
What payment methods do you accept? ↓
For international clients — PayPal, Wise, and Fiverr platform payment. For local Philippine clients — GCash and bank transfer. Payment terms will be discussed before starting any project.
Can you handle multiple projects at once? ↓
Yes, but I'm transparent about my availability. If I have a heavy workload, I'll let you know upfront so we can set realistic expectations and timelines.
Do you work with clients outside the Philippines? ↓
Absolutely. I work with clients globally — US, UK, Australia, and beyond. Fluent in English and experienced with remote collaboration. Time zone differences are never a problem.
